Types of Room Available

Standard Room

Our Standard Rooms offer a comfortable and affordable stay with essential amenities. Each room features a cozy bed, a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, and a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries. Ideal for solo travelers or couples looking for a simple yet pleasant accommodation experience.

Deluxe Room

Experience luxury and comfort in our Deluxe Rooms. These rooms are more spacious and come with enhanced amenities such as a king-sized bed, a mini-fridge, a work desk, and a seating area. Enjoy a relaxing stay with premium bedding, a larger flat-screen TV, and a well-appointed bathroom with upscale toiletries.

Price: ₱20,000.00

Suite Room

Indulge in the ultimate luxury with our Suite Rooms. These exclusive rooms offer the finest accommodations with separate living and sleeping areas, a large bathroom with a jacuzzi, and a private balcony with stunning views. Perfect for those seeking a lavish stay, our Suite Rooms also include complimentary breakfast, a mini-bar, and personalized services to ensure a memorable experience.
